Chicken Pot Pie

First, the filling must be made by boiling the chicken with the carrots, peas and celery. The onions are then cooked in butter until translucent, then the flour and seasoning is added. Add chicken broth and milk, and simmer until filling is thick. Next, the pie will be filled and baked. Place the chicken-vegetable mixture in an unbaked pie crust, and pour the chicken broth mixture over it. Cover the top crust, seal edges, and cut slits in the top to allow steam to escape. Bake until pastry is golden brown and filling is bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless cubed chicken breasts
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Celery seed
  • Chicken broth
  • Milk
  • Two unbaked pie crusts

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Combine chicken, carrots, peas and celery in saucepan. Add water to cover and boil for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and drain.
  3. Melt butter in another saucepan while chicken is cooking over medium heat. Add onion and cook until soft and translucent, 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed.
  4. Slowly stir in chicken broth and milk
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until thick, 5 to 10 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  6. Add chicken and vegetables to bottom pie crust. Pour hot liquid mixture over top.
  7. Cover with top crust, seal edges, and cut away excess dough. Make slits in top crust to allow steam to escape.
  8. Bake in oven until pastry is golden brown and filling is bubbly, 30 to 35 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
