In N Out Style Burger

The famous burger chain In N Out is renowned for its affordable yet delicious burgers. The killing point of the burger is the secret sauce, which unites all of the ingredients well. This recipe details the production of the burger as well as the crucial secret sauce with it.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef 80/20
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • American cheese
  • Sliced tomato
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Sliced white onion
  • Hamburger buns
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Sweet pickle relish
  • White vinegar

Steps

  1. Form the beef into 4 balls of a quarter of a pound each.
  2. Combine sauce ingredients (last 4 on the list) in a bowl. Make sure they are mixed evenly.
  3. Preheat skillet over medium high heat. Drizzle oil in pan to prevent burger from sticking.
  4. Toast buns on preheated pan for 2 minutes. Set aside.
  5. Press down beef balls into flat patties after placing on hot pan.
  6. Cook patties for 2 minutes until color changes. Flip, but do not smash down again. Place 2 slices of cheese atop each patty.
  7. Cook patties for 1 more minute until cheese melts.
  8. Assemble burgers. Spread sauce on bottom of toasted top bun. Add lettuce, tomatoes, and onions along with the patty. Enjoy.
